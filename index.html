About Me

William Harrison born March 11, 1985 in Greensboro, NC. Now lets skip ahead after High school William graduated from UNC-Greensboro in 2007 with a bachelor degree in Small Business. Taking his new small business degree he went to work for his family business at Graham Sporting Goods in Burlington, NC.

5 years later he moved back to Greensboro and started the website GrahamSG.com. Where he sells hats, socks, shirts and other sporting goods apparel online. The site has been gradually growing over the last few years and is becoming one of the larger dealers of custom hats in the southeast. Looking to expand his website and brand William is taking a coding course hoping on taking is website to the next level.

What does William enjoy doing in his free time?

William has a wife named Katelin, a 3 year old daughter named Lilly and a Karelian Bear Dog named Bella. I suggest googling about Karelian Bear Dogs. They are rare and unique breed of dogs. William spends his limited free time hanging out with his family and hiking in the mountains.
